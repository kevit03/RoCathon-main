Plain English

Core terms without the industry jargon.

Atlas Score

A screening score that blends creator fit, query fit, audience fit, and commercial quality.

GMV

Gross merchandise value. In simple terms, how much product value a creator helped drive.

Projected score

RoC’s forward-looking business signal. It estimates how commercially strong a creator is likely to be.

Semantic fit

How well a creator’s content meaning matches the brief, not just exact keywords.

Audience fit

How closely a creator’s audience lines up with the brand’s target demographic profile.

Why it matters

Atlas helps teams avoid high-reach creators who are commercially or contextually wrong for the brand.
